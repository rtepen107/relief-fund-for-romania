Design Challenge

The first step was to determine the design problem. Using the IDEO design challenge template, a more refined problem was found.

What is the problen you're trying to solve?

Support the less-fortunate groups in Romania.

Frame it as a design question.

How can the website promate aid to less fortunate groups in Romania?

The Ultimate Impact

Improve the quality of life and solve needs of various groups of less-fortunate in Romania.

Possible Solutions

Information prioritising

Call to action(s)

Visual heiarchy

Simplicity of design and straightforward navigation

Use of pictures and iconography

Context and Restraints

As a UK based charity for Romania, communication can be an issue.

There is a lack of current information and photos.

A single charity directing for multiple projects and causes.

Restated Design Question

How might the website better direct potential donors to various projects and information effectively?
